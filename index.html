Canadian Certified Pedorthists - C.Ped (C)

Canadian Certifed Pedothists are foot orthotic and orthopaedic footwear experts. They are one of the few heathcare professionals trained in the assessment of lower limb anatomy and muscle and joint function.

With specialized education and training in foot orthotics and footwear, Canadian Certified Pedorthists help to alleviate pain, abnormalities and debilitating conditions of the lower limbs and feet.
